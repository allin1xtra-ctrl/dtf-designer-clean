How to use in Shopify:
1. Open Online Store > Themes > Edit code in your Flora theme.
2. Upload or paste the files into the matching sections and assets folders.
3. Add DTF product designer using Add section on a product template.
4. If needed, merge templates/flora-product-template-snippet.json into your Flora product template JSON.
5. Set your Custom Drip, Custom Transfers, Gang Sheets, and Starter Packs URLs in the section settings.
